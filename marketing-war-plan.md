**The Formula:** Give value 3x before asking once.

**For CourtLab:**
- **Jab 1:** Free training tip with no pitch
- **Jab 2:** Behind-the-scenes content (why we built this)
- **Jab 3:** Coach testimonial or player success story
- **Right Hook:** "Link in bio for early access" or "Join 500+ coaches tracking their players"

**Current Status:** ‚ö†Ô∏è NOT OPTIMIZED ‚Äî We're doing 1:1 jab-to-hook ratio in current calendar

**Fix:** Restructure content to 3:1 ratio minimum

### Native Content Per Platform
**Gary V Rule:** Don't cross-post the same content. Each platform has different "context."

| Platform | Context | CourtLab Strategy |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Instagram** | Visual inspiration, aspiration | Before/after player metrics, high-quality training shots |
| **TikTok** | Entertainment, education fast | 15-sec "Did you know?" + "Track it with CourtLab" |
| **Twitter/X** | Conversation, thought leadership | Hot takes on youth sports data, coach threads |
| **Facebook** | Community, events, parents | Easter Classic event, parent education, longer-form |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional, B2B | Club partnership case studies, "I built this" founder story |
| **YouTube** | Deep education | Full combine walkthroughs, coach tutorials |

**Current Status:** ‚ùå FAILING ‚Äî Same captions cross-posted to IG + FB

### The "Thumbnail + Hook" Rule
**Gary V:** "The biggest missed opportunity is thumbnails and the first 3 seconds."

**CourtLab Action:**
- Every Reel needs a scroll-stopping first frame (text overlay: "Your vertical jump is a lie")
- First 2 seconds = pattern interrupt (not logo, not "Hey guys")
- Example hooks:
  - "That coach is wrong about your kid. Here's the data..."
  - "I tested 200 kids. 80% couldn't touch their combine numbers from last year."
  - "The $7.99 app lied to this player. Here's what actually happened."
