```
-free
-youtube
-nba
-highlights
-live stream
-video streaming
-college basketball (unless targeting coaches recruiting)
-basketball news
-basketball scores

-fantasy
```

**Why:** These searchers aren't looking to download a coaching/training app; they want content or gambling. Low intent = high waste.
