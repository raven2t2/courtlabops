## Part 2: Keyword Categorization & Strategy

### A. BRAND TERMS (Defense + Quick Wins)
**Purpose:** Capture people already looking for CourtLab

```
CourtLab
CourtLab basketball
CourtLab trainer
CourtLab app
```

**Strategy:**
- Bid high ($1.50-2.50) â€” these are super-qualified
- Use defensive bids against competitor brand search
- Low volume, high conversion

---

### B. COMPETITOR TERMS (Offense)
**Purpose:** Convert people choosing between apps

**Primary Competitors:**
1. **HomeCourt** (market leader in AU, $200/year)
   - Keywords: "homecourt alternative", "basketball app like homecourt", "homecourt vs"
   - Bid Strategy: $1.20-2.00 (undercut on price messaging)
   - Ad Message: "Professional combine timing without the subscription cost"

2. **Sideline** (popular for game analysis)
   - Keywords: "sideline basketball app", "sideline alternative"
   - Bid Strategy: $1.10-1.80
   - Ad Message: "Game analysis + player development in one app"

3. **Coach's Eye** (video analysis specialist)
   - Keywords: "coach's eye alternative", "basketball video analysis app"
   - Bid Strategy: $1.00-1.50
   - Ad Message: "Video analysis with verified combine data"

**Strategy:**
- Bid lower than brand terms ($1.20-2.00)
- Emphasize unique value: "Verified timing data" (not AI guesses)
- Test headline: "CourtLab: Combine times coaches trust"

---

### C. GENERIC BASKETBALL TRAINING TERMS
**Purpose:** Own broad searches from coaches/players looking to improve

**Primary Keywords:**
- basketball training app
- basketball stats app
- basketball coaching app
- basketball player development
- basketball drill app
- basketball workout app

**Strategy:**
- **Mid-tier bids** ($1.50-2.80): These are high volume but lower intent
- Target coaches (not casual players)
- Use AI negative keywords: -free, -nba, -highlights, -youtube
- Test ad messaging: "Improve combine times" vs "Track player progress"

---

### D. YOUTH/CLUB/SCHOOL BASKETBALL TERMS
**Purpose:** Reach Australian youth basketball ecosystem (biggest growth market)

**Primary Keywords:**
- youth basketball training app
- basketball club management app
- school basketball team app
- junior basketball coach app
- basketball combine australia
- australian basketball stats

**Strategy:**
- **Moderate bids** ($1.30-2.30): High intent in AU market, good conversion
- Target parents + coaches of youth players
- Messaging: "Prepare kids for next level" + "Combine-ready timing"
- Geographic targeting: Focus on Adelaide, Melbourne, Sydney metro areas

---

### E. PLAYER-FOCUSED (Secondary Audience)
**Purpose:** Players looking to improve shooting, stats, performance

**Keywords:**
- basketball shooting tracker
- basketball performance app
- basketball workout app
- basketball skills app

**Strategy:**
- **Lower bids** ($1.20-2.00): Player intent is different from coach intent
- Test messaging: "Track every shot. See progress." vs "Get recruited"
- Consider: CourtLab's primary user is coaches, not players â€” but players drive family adoption
